A rapidly emerging real estate zone, a limited-time entry window, and a strategic location shaping Karachi’s next big growth corridor.

By Lateef Properties | Updated January 2026

A Neighborhood Quietly Turning Into a Prime Opportunity

Model Colony Malir has evolved over the past decade — but 2026 marks a turning point. With major public and private development around the Malir Expressway, Airport vicinity, and surrounding commercial belts, the locality is transitioning into a fully connected and highly livable zone.

For investors, this is the moment before prices climb into the next bracket.

1. The Malir Expressway Effect

The biggest catalyst behind the area’s rise is the Malir Expressway, which has dramatically cut travel times to DHA, Clifton, Shahrah-e-Faisal, and Karsaz. This single factor has shifted buyer interest towards Malir’s neighborhoods — especially Model Colony.

Connectivity drives value. And Model Colony is now one of Karachi’s most connected mid-range localities.

2. A Sharp Rise in Population and Housing Demand

Families are moving into the area because of:

  • Lower per-square-foot prices compared to Gulshan and Jauhar
  • Safe residential pockets with established utilities
  • Growing commercial activity
  • Nearby schools, parks, clinics, and markets

This steady demand is fueling property appreciation across apartments, portions, and plots.

3. The Price Gap Won’t Last Long

Model Colony currently sits in a “value sweet spot” — still affordable, but rising fast. However, the gap between Malir and central Karachi zones is closing quickly as development accelerates.

This makes the present moment a limited-time entry window for investors looking for maximum ROI.

4. A New Commercial Wave

Retail chains, clinics, and local businesses are expanding into the area due to its location advantage and growing population. The increased commercial presence boosts both residential demand and long-term property value.
