Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about Tulip Comfort apartments

Why should I invest during the pre-launch phase?

Entering during the pre-launch phase is the most lucrative time to buy. It allows you to lock in the asset at a heavily discounted introductory rate (saving up to 1.3 to 2.25 Million PKR depending on the unit) before prices are officially revised upwards at launch. You also get first pick of the best floor level and apartment options.

What are the layout options and sizes?

Tulip Comfort is designed for spacious living, avoiding cramped layouts. We offer expansive 4, 6 & 7-room apartments, alongside strictly limited exclusive duplex designs for maximum luxury.

What is the payment structure?

We offer a highly flexible 5-Year (60-month) easy installment plan. You can secure your unit with an accessible initial down payment, allowing you to manage your cash flow while your asset appreciates in value. Submit the form to get the exact payment breakdowns for each unit type.

How can I view the exact floor plans and prices?

Because pre-launch inventory moves rapidly, we provide the most up-to-date availability, floor plans, and pricing directly via WhatsApp. Click "Get Floor Plans & Consultation" to receive the brochure instantly.
