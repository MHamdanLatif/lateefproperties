Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about Rim Jhim Villas

What properties are currently available in Rim Jhim Villas?

We currently have two highly sought-after ready units available: a spacious 165 SYD double-storey independent house, and a luxurious 3-bed (3 BDD) apartment. Both are ready for immediate possession.

Is Rim Jhim Villas a secure community?

Yes, Rim Jhim Villas is a strictly gated community featuring a secure boundary wall, 24/7 CCTV surveillance, and trained security personnel at the entrance to ensure a safe environment for your family.

Where is Rim Jhim Villas located?

It is located in Gulshan-e-Usman Housing Society, Scheme 33, Karachi. The location provides peaceful residential living while maintaining fast and easy access to the Karachi-Hyderabad Motorway and main city routes.

Are there amenities within the society boundary?

Absolutely. The society is self-sustaining and family-oriented, featuring a dedicated mosque, community parks, children's play areas, and essential commercial shops directly within the gated walls.
